About Crista

I help women make fat loss feel livable again.

I've been a fitness trainer, fat loss coach, and nutrition consultant for almost 18 years. In that time, I've worked with women from all different walks of life and starting points. One thing is always true: knowing what to do is not the same as being able to do it.

The women have never been lazy, and they're never undisciplined. In fact, they try harder than most people ever will. The problem is not effort. It was just that nobody had helped them find what works specifically for them: their hormones, their schedules, what's happening inside their head, and how they're thinking about things.

That is where I come in. I understand this personally. I myself have a genetic predisposition to obesity, so I know what it's like to struggle with these things and to have to learn how to rethink food. This is the work that I care about, and I truly believe that things don't have to be so hard.
